Theory Comic





"Cristina Through the Liquid Crystal Display" explores the concept of an algo-numeric double--one's code nym. Assembled entirely with found imagery, mostly via Google Image searches of my name, it is a remix of Lewis Carroll's Alice Through the Looking Glass employing stills from Channel 4's 1998 television version directed by John Henderson and starring Kate Beckinsale.

The comic was an assignment for Dr. Jon V. McKenzie's Bascom Seminar "Orality, Literacy, and Digitality" at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, Spring 2013.
