Rooftop Rambles

A blog about the Rooftop Ramble, an indoor/outdoor garden exhibit on the roof of Madison Children's Museum (MCM). Created when I was an Education Program Assistant at MCM at the request of the exhibit coordinator, Julie King, the blog was updated by several staff with current activities and photos.
